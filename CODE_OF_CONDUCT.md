# <SAMP> Our Pledge

<SAMP>
  
**We pledge to ensure a harassment‑free, inclusive community for all, regardless of identity or background, and to act with respect, empathy, and integrity to foster openness, diversity, and healthy collaboration.**

* **Standards** – Positive conduct includes empathy, respect, constructive feedback, accountability, and prioritizing community benefit. Unacceptable conduct includes harassment, sexualized language/imagery, trolling, insults, political/personal attacks, or publishing private information.  
* **Scope & Enforcement** – Applies to all community spaces and official representation. Community leaders may remove or reject contributions, enforce standards, and investigate reports while respecting reporter privacy.  
* **Consequences** – Violations handled progressively: *Correction* (private warning), *Warning* (restricted interaction), *Temporary Ban* (suspension), *Permanent Ban* (removal for repeated/severe violations).
